COMPANY PROFILE.

BM TELECOMMS AND NETWORKS CO LTD is an ICT Telecom and Security Class 1 contractor registered by both the Contractors Registration Board and Tanzania Communication Regulatory Authority. A Company that has been operating since 23rd February 2004, as a Data Voice and Networking integrator has undergone striking evolution and growth over time to become a provider of a wide variety of communications products and services.

BM TELECOMMS AND NETWORKS CO LTD have developed a reputation for excellence in supply, installation, and maintenance across the spectrum of products in the industry of ICT Telecommunication, Security systems, and related fields. Our services span the entire lifecycle of IT Infrastructure, Consulting, Installation, Sales of IT Equipment, Networking Equipment, Maintenance of IT Infrastructure, Turnkey Projects on IT Infrastructure, Disaster Recovery. Our business goal is to develop and create cost-effective solutions, from the design phase through installation, and provide maintenance and support thereafter. We take a team approach with our customers to help them identify possible solutions, evaluate those for effectiveness and appropriateness to the problem, and then implement the most favorable alternatives. Our decision to represent multiple vendors increases our alternatives for developing the best solutions for our customers. Our clientele includes organizations from various domains such as Government institutions, Telecommunication Companies, Media, Banking, Healthcare, ISPs, Network Integrators, Networking Consultants, and Educational Institutes. Our clientele ranges from Large Enterprises, Small and Medium Enterprises to Service Providers, Small Offices, and Home Offices.
